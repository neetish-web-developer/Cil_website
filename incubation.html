What is the Selection Process

  1. Preliminary scrutiny of applications
  2. Shortlisted applicants pitch to expert panel

The pitching may result in:

    • Selection for incubation / pre-incubation program (6 months, extendable up to 12 months)
    • Suggestions to improve your business plan so that you can revise the plans and apply later
  • Offer of incubation / pre-incubation to selected applicants
  • Accept offer, Sign agreements and Get going with your venture
